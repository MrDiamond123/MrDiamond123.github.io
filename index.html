Projects @ iTEAM

Ultimate Sales Challenge

Ultimate Sales Challenge is the first project we do each year in which we had to try to sell food, drinks, a non-food item, and a pay-to-participate activity (first year, when programming and enterpreneurship were a combined class, also included programming developing a game in Unity). First year, our team won the challenge! Second year, I wore a inflatable Pikachu Among Us costume (we did not win)!

Corporate Battle

Corporate Battle is the second project we work on. It's a competition between the AM and PM classes, where we have to put on an event with Oakland County Parks to both encourage job applications and to have a fun events for families to attend! For the first year, we had various Unity games that the programmers developed and I provided programming and technical assistance. I had also developed a career quiz to help the people seeking jobs figure out which job would be the best for them. Second year, I had also consulted with the programmer who developed a new version of the career quiz. First year, we had won against PM and second year, while we lost, it was extremely close.

Charity Golf Outing

Our last project we do each year is our Charity Golf Outing where we (both AM and PM) work together to try to raise money for our chosen charities and our student club DECA.
